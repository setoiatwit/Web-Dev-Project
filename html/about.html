About

Our Story

Due to Covid and its ramifications both socially and economically, depression has been on a steady rise since early 2020. Due to this increase, it has also become increasingly hard to find available emotional and medical support. This led to an increase in demand, but lack of supply has left a lot of people feeling helpless or stranded, causing a significant decrease in quality of life. We wanted to create a convenient and open-source website that allows anyone to anonymously seek mental or emotional support from a certified therapist. We hope our website can remove the fear and hesitation from starting conversations about mental health and substance use. Our webpage allows you to post and view forums so that you can quickly get help from a certified therapist or from someone who may be going through the same struggles. Having a forum creates a safe space for all since it allows you to share your experiences and learn from someone else's story.

Our Target

Our target is people who are struggling with mental disorders, emotional support, and substance abuse. Our webpage is made for those who do not have a platform or the resources to seek help.

Our History

Web Therapy was created in 2022 by Ian Seto, Cuong Le, Spencer Janse, and Will Tran. We wanted to create a website that would be able to provide help to others. We also wanted our website to focus on emotional health and mental disorders since we believe it is a big ongoing issue in America today.
