Information

Globably, 800,000 people die a year from suicide, which doubles the number of yearly homicide. Suicide is the number one leading causes of death in young people.

In 2020 alone, about 1 in five adults experinced mental health issues, 1 in 6 young people experienced a major depressive episode, and 1 in 20 Americans lived with a serious mental illness, such as schizophrenia, bipolar disorder, or major depression.

Since the pandemic, there have been 6.16 million deaths worldwide and 982,000 deaths in the United States alone. If you have lost someone due to related medical issues, you are not alone and there are people in similar situations.

Our goal for this website is to spread awareness for mental health issues for anyone going through a rough time in their life and need people to talk to as our website offers a forum for those in need to speak their mind or to offer advice that may help others in similar situations.
